What Should I Wear Tomorrow? Your Fashion Forecast For NYC, LA, And The Rest Of The US

Share Tweet Pin it

Ryan Pierse/Getty Images News/Getty Images

Though some regions of the United States may be thinking “Spring weather? What spring weather?”, it’s still a joy to be on the other side of The Worst Winter Ever (aka the fallout from the Polar Vortex). Slowly but surely, the mercury is rising across the country, from Los Angeles’s consistently balmy weather to New York’s, well, mostly sunny skies. 

If you’re located in one of those lucky cities with warmth to spare, however, don’t forget to bring along an extra umbrella or scarf. The weather is still temperamental this time of year, and it’s not exactly amusing to be caught in a storm without your umbrella, or experience a chilly gust of wind when you’ve left your jacket at home. Furthermore, Weather.com predicts that much of the East Coast and parts of the Mid East are due for thunderstorms over the course of this week. 

Thankfully, inclement weather doesn’t have to be a sartorial drag anymore due to the influx of adorable accoutrements from scarves to sweet galoshes. And the ensemble underneath all of that weather gear definitely doesn’t have to reflect the dreary forecast. So hold on to your umbrellas and pack accessories that can carry you through any type of weather, because the remainder of this week is bound to be a bit stormy. Here’s what you should wear in your area on Thursday.
