"Too often engagement doesn't bring customers closer to conversion."

- Forrester, June 2012

Conversion is 6x lower for social traffic.


Online retailers are missing the opportunity of social networks. While search and email marketing are driving clear value, too few can say social media has been effective at converting traffic into paying customers.


Social visitors behave differently.


People reached on social networks are in a browsing mode. While social media is a growing source of product discovery for consumers, these "browsers" are not actively looking for a product, and they're not thinking about making a purchase.


Dead-ends create leaky buckets.


Today's eCommerce experience is not optimized for the "browser" consumer mindset. Traditional product pages do not facilitate product discovery. Visitors who aren't ready to buy have little choice but to leave.

Opportunity for re-engagement is limited.


A missed sale often means no follow up. Purchase preferences are unknown, and without email capture, one-to-one communication is impossible.
